BBQ Chicken Pizza

This homemade dough comes fluffy when baked and brings more sensory stimulation to the mouth when consuming this pizza. The barbecue sauce on top is neither over powering nor too little in each bite. It brings out the full juices in the chicken and could not go any better dipped in ranch. This pizza is rather cooked in a charcoal oven to bring out a more smoked taste to the chicken. Multiple cheese top the entire pizza and brings a peppery taste to the overall product

Taco Pizza

A classic oversized pizza that servers up to 5 to 6 people that will remind you of hot a summer day eating nachos and drinking a Modelo. This pizza is topped with a lot of cheese as well as salsa with a bonus of taco seasoning to bring out the spices present in a traditional Mexican meal. The flavor has been compared to some of Chef Ramsey Gordon's best dishes

Vegetarian Pizza
